Assume that we use a SMF with $D=16.7 ps/(nm\cdot km)$, length $L$ and  attenuation $\alpha_\text{DCF}=0.2\text{dB/km}$ for transmission. To compensate dispersion locally, we make use of a DCF
\footnote{
Dispersion-Compensating Fiber
}
 with dispersion parameter $D_\text{DCF}=-4.84 ps/(nm\cdot km)$, attenuation $\alpha_\text{DCF}=0.22\text{dB/km}$ and length $L_\text{DCF}$ which is deployed after the SMF. The DCF compensates the dispersion completely, hence 
$$
D\cdot L+D_\text{DCF}\cdot L_\text{DCF}=0.
$$
The signal is allowed to drop $20\text{dB}$ before it is amplified.
Calculate the maximum length of signal transmission without amplifiers. Find $L$ and $L_\text{DCF}$ at this maximum transmission length.
